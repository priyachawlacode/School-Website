SCHOOL HOUSES

We have four school houses with house captains from year 11 and sports captains from Year 9:-

Every child is assigned a house in KS1. Each week the house points from each class are added. The house with the highest number is rewarded with the house point cup in Friday's 'Celebration Assembly'.

This is recorded on the House Point wall in the school hall and on the school website.

In the summer term Sports Day is all about our house TEAMS - each child represents their own house. In every event the child that comes 1st, 2nd or 3rd is awarded points and the house with the most points, at the end of the day, wins the Sports Cup.



House Captains and Sports Captains are chosen each year - they are all in Year 9 and 11 and are fantastic role models for the school.
